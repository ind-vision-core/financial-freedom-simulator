The Rules of Reality

  1. 01

    Start early

    Time does significantly heavier lifting than raw capital due to compounding. Every year you delay doubles the effort required.

    Own productive assets

    Cash is a melting ice cube in a warm room. Protect your purchasing power by owning global infrastructure, businesses, or gold.

    The market is a sophisticated machine designed to transfer wealth from the impatient to the patient. Extreme adrenaline ruins portfolios.
